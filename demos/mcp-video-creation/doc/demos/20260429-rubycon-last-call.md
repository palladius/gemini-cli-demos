# Storyboard: Rubycon 2026 - Last Call!

## Style
Cinematic, fast-paced, and high-energy. Blending "Epic Roman History" with "Modern Tech Hype". High contrast, vibrant colors, and a sense of "Fear Of Missing Out" (FOMO).

## Characters
*   **The Centurion:** A Roman soldier in full armor, but wearing high-tech VR goggles and a Ruby-red cape.
*   **The Coder:** A developer frantically typing on a glowing mechanical keyboard, surrounded by Roman columns.

---

## Scene 1: The Alarm (00:00 - 00:06)
*   **Visuals:** A close-up of a Roman sundial. As the shadow moves rapidly, it transforms into a digital countdown clock. The background shows a bustling Roman forum that slowly overlays with a modern tech conference floor.
*   **VEO Prompt:** "Extreme close-up of an ancient Roman sundial, the shadow spinning fast, morphing into a glowing digital countdown timer. Cinematic lighting, dust motes in the air, 8k resolution."
*   **Music:** Fast-paced, ticking clock sound layered with a deep, cinematic synth bass.
*   **Text Overlay:** "TEMPUS FUGIT. TIME IS RUNNING OUT."

## Scene 2: The Command (00:06 - 00:12)
*   **Visuals:** A Roman Centurion stands atop the Arch of Augustus in Rimini. He raises a glowing red sword (shaped like a Ruby) towards the sky. In the background, modern drones fly in formation, forming the Ruby logo.
*   **VEO Prompt:** "A Roman Centurion in polished armor standing on an ancient stone arch, holding a glowing red crystal sword aloft. Modern drones in the sunset sky form a ruby shape. Epic, wide shot, cinematic."
*   **Music:** Orchestral brass hits, heroic and urgent.
*   **Text Overlay:** "THE RUBICON AWAITS."

## Scene 3: The Gathering (00:12 - 00:18)
*   **Visuals:** A fast montage of developers in togas clinking glasses of wine/coffee, looking at a giant holographic screen showing Ruby code. The "Sala Quarzo" is packed and the energy is electric.
*   **VEO Prompt:** "A diverse crowd of people in white Roman togas and tech lanyards, cheering and holding glowing glass mugs. A giant holographic code display in the background. High energy, motion blur, 4k."
*   **Music:** Upbeat, driving electronic dance music (EDM) with a Roman choir melody.
*   **Text Overlay:** "JOIN THE ELITE."

## Scene 4: The Final Call (00:18 - 00:25)
*   **Visuals:** The camera flies through the Rimini coastline at high speed towards a "SOLD OUT" sign that hasn't quite lit up yet. A hand reaches out to click a "BUY TICKET" button on a floating stone tablet.
*   **VEO Prompt:** "FPV drone shot flying over the Rimini beach at sunset towards a floating stone tablet with a glowing 'BUY TICKETS' button. Cinematic, high speed, vibrant colors."
*   **Music:** A final, powerful musical swell that ends abruptly with a heartbeat sound.
*   **Text Overlay:** "LAST TICKETS REMAINING. RUBYCON.IT"
