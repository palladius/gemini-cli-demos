# Storyboard: Rubycon 2026 - The Bluebird Legacy

## Style
Whimsical, high-tech, and magical. Vibrant colors with a soft, cinematic glow. The transition from Blue to Red should be visually stunning and fluid.

## Characters
*   **The Bluebirds:** A flock of small, vibrant blue birds (reminiscent of the early Twitter bird).
*   **The Architects:** A diverse group of developers standing on the banks of the Rubicon, looking at the horizon with hope.

---

## Scene 1: The Migration (00:00 - 00:06)
*   **Visuals:** A beautiful sunset over a peaceful river (the Rubicon). A massive flock of small, bright blue birds flies across the screen in a graceful formation. The atmosphere is calm and full of wonder.
*   **VEO Prompt:** "Cinematic wide shot of a peaceful river at sunset in the Italian countryside. A flock of hundreds of small, vibrant blue birds flies gracefully across the orange and purple sky. High quality, 8k, soft lighting."
*   **Music:** Gentle, ethereal acoustic guitar with light ambient bird chirps.
*   **Text Overlay:** "A LEGACY BUILT ON RUBY."

## Scene 2: The Transformation (00:06 - 00:12)
*   **Visuals:** The birds dive towards the sparkling water of the river. As they touch the surface, they don't submerge; they transform into glowing, red pentagonal diamonds (Rubies) that float and shimmer, illuminating the river bank.
*   **VEO Prompt:** "Macro shot of vibrant blue birds diving into a river. Upon contact with the water, they burst into shimmering red pentagonal diamonds. The water glows red. Magical, surreal, 8k resolution."
*   **Music:** A magical chimes sound effect transitioning into an uplifting, rhythmic electronic beat.
*   **Text Overlay:** "BORN FROM INNOVATION."

## Scene 3: The Discovery (00:12 - 00:18)
*   **Visuals:** Diverse developers on the beach in Rimini. They reach out and the floating rubies land softly in their palms, turning into holographic lines of code. They smile and share the light with each other.
*   **VEO Prompt:** "Diverse software developers on a beach at dusk. Shimmering red rubies float into their hands and morph into glowing holographic code. Warm, community atmosphere, 4k."
*   **Music:** High-energy, optimistic electronic music (the "Rubycon Theme").
*   **Text Overlay:** "JOIN THE COMMUNITY."

## Scene 4: The Invitation (00:18 - 00:25)
*   **Visuals:** The camera pulls back to show the Rimini skyline. The floating rubies gather in the sky to form the Rubycon 2026 logo. The "Last Tickets" message appears with a soft, pulsing red glow.
*   **VEO Prompt:** "Sweeping aerial shot of the Rimini coastline at night. Thousands of glowing red rubies in the sky cluster together to form a large pentagonal diamond logo. Cinematic, 8k."
*   **Music:** A powerful musical swell that ends with a clear, resonant chime.
*   **Text Overlay:** "LAST TICKETS REMAINING. RUBYCON.IT"
